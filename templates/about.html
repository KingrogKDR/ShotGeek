About ShotGeek


Welcome to ShotGeek, a companion app designed for basketball lovers who enjoy analyzing shot data over a shot of espresso! We strive to merge our passion for the game with data-driven insights, allowing fans to easily explore NBA statistics, player comparisons, and team performances.

Whether you're a casual fan or a statistics enthusiast, ShotGeek is tailored to offer a convenient and comprehensive breakdown of your favorite players and teams. Delve into detailed player stats, career insights, trends, and team data from across the NBA all in one place.

For Basketball Lovers, By Basketball Lovers:

ShotGeek was established and is maintained by dedicated developers and NBA enthusiasts. If you'd like to support us, partner with us, or send us a message, please email us at info@shotgeek.com
